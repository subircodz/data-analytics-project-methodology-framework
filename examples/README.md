# DAPM Examples

Welcome to the **Examples** section of the **Data Analytics Project Methodology (DAPM) Framework**.

You may have expected to find completed documentation examples here.

Instead, you'll find something more valuable.

DAPM follows an **evidence-first philosophy**.

Rather than creating fictional examples, every template in this framework is being validated through real analytics projects. Once validated, those projects become the official reference implementations of the framework.

---

# Why are there no example documents?

Every organization is different.

A warehouse, hospital, retail business, logistics company, bank, or manufacturing plant all have unique business problems, stakeholders, datasets, KPIs, and analytical objectives.

For that reason, DAPM intentionally does **not** provide generic "completed examples" for its templates.

Instead, the framework provides:

- Standardized documentation templates
- A repeatable analytics methodology
- Real-world validation projects that demonstrate how the templates are applied in practice

This approach keeps the framework flexible while ensuring that every published example has been tested in an actual business scenario.

# Our Philosophy

> **Business First. Evidence Always.**

Every project tells a different story.

DAPM provides the methodology.

The projects demonstrate how that methodology is applied.

The goal is not to teach analysts **what to think**, but to provide a structured process that helps them think systematically, document professionally, and deliver evidence-based business decisions.
