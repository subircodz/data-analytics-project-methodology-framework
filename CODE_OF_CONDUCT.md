# Code of Conduct

## Commitment

The DAPM project is committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone.

Whether you are reporting an issue, suggesting an improvement, or contributing documentation, please treat others with professionalism and respect.

---

## Expected Behavior

Contributors are encouraged to:

* Be respectful and constructive.
* Focus discussions on improving the methodology.
* Welcome different perspectives and experiences.
* Accept feedback professionally.
* Support evidence-based discussions.
* Help maintain a positive learning environment.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

* Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
* Personal attacks or insulting language.
* Deliberate misinformation.
* Spam or disruptive behavior.
* Unprofessional or disrespectful communication.

---

## Guiding Principle

DAPM values evidence over opinion.

When proposing improvements, contributors are encouraged to support their suggestions with practical examples, case studies, or project experience whenever possible.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove contributions or restrict participation that violates this Code of Conduct.

---

Thank you for helping make DAPM a respectful and collaborative open-source project.
